JSC and JPL Astronaut Boot Testing | April 24, 2025

The objective of this project was to apply thermal load to the astronaut boot, isolated inside the chamber at cryogenic temperatures and high vacuum, in “flight like” conditions to measure its thermal gradient. Modeling the boot’s thermal profile will help NASA to develop an industry-standard astronaut boot thermal requirement for future missions. The unit under test consisted of a mannequin foot coupled with fluid lines that conducted heat, mimicking a human’s foot, surrounded by about 9 different external layers (including the bladder, insole, outsole) that make up the astronaut boot.

Mujeres Destacadas was founded nearly three decades ago to celebrate the achievements of Outstanding Latina Women in the tri-state area and highlight the positive contributions they have made in their community.
